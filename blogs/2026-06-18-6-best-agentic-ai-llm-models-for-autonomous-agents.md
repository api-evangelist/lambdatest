A comparison of the 6 best agentic AI LLM models for autonomous agents in 2026, ranging from GPT-5.5 to Claude Opus 4.8, with guidance on how to test each for reliable tool use. An agentic AI LLM is a model tuned to plan, call tools, and run multi-step tasks with little human input, running a loop of reading the goal, choosing an action, calling a tool or API, reading the result, and repeating until done.
